About

Building Functions.
Enabling Leaders.

I am a Chartered Accountant and Certified Internal Auditor who builds and betters risk, audit, and strategic functions at the intersection of technology, emerging risks, and sustainability. I have built new practices from the ground up, bettered existing ones under regulatory scrutiny, and served as a trusted strategic partner to executives, Boards, and Audit Committees.

My work sits at the crossroads of strategy and execution. I design first-time programs, establish scalable infrastructure, and roll up my sleeves to get things done. I report directly to leadership, challenge constructively, and translate complex risks into clear, actionable insights. I am both a strategic thinker and a hands-on enabler—a force multiplier for executives and Boards navigating complexity.

I help organizations of all sizes and maturities scan the horizon for known and unknown risks, strengthen governance across jurisdictions, and scale with guardrails while keeping pace with innovation. I apply deep technical rigor with business practicality to help leaders move faster, smarter, and safer.
